About Dr. Kiruba Shankar

Patient Information

Before Your Appointment

  • Bring all previous medical records and imaging (CT/MRI scans)
  • List all current medications and allergies
  • Prepare a list of symptoms and questions
  • Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time
  • Carry your insurance information if applicable

Pre-Surgical Instructions

  • Complete pre-operative tests as advised
  • Follow fasting instructions (typically 8 hours)
  • Stop certain medications as directed (blood thinners, etc.)
  • Arrange for a companion to accompany you
  • Pack essential items for hospital stay if required

Post-Operative Care

  • Follow all medication schedules precisely
  • Attend all follow-up appointments
  • Keep surgical site clean and dry
  • Watch for signs of infection or complications
  • Gradually resume activities as advised
